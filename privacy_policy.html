Privacy Policy

StreetVine

Effective date: August 22, 2025

StreetVine (“we”, “our”, “us”) built the StreetVine app as a free app. This page explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our Service.

Information We Collect & How We Use It

Camera. We request camera access so you can capture and upload event posters/photos inside the app. Photos are uploaded to our secure Firebase backend. We do not access your camera or photos without your action.

Location. With your permission, we may collect your location to show nearby events and power search/filters. You can disable location at any time in device settings.

Storage. Uploaded images are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage. We don’t share your personal files outside the app.

Authentication. We use Firebase Authentication to create and manage your account (e.g., email and basic profile data).

Notifications. With consent, we send event-related push notifications using Firebase Cloud Messaging.

Third-Party Services

We use the following providers, which may process limited data to provide the Service:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore/Storage, Messaging)
  • Google Places API (location autocomplete and place details)

See Google’s Privacy Policy for more information.
