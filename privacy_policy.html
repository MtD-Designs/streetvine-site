Privacy Policy StreetVine

Effective date: August 22, 2025 • Developer: MtD Designs

StreetVine (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides a community events app. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using StreetVine you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Account & Contact Info: Email and basic profile details used for sign-in and friend features (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Approximate or Precise Location (optional): If you allow it, we use your location to show nearby events and improve search. You can disable this in iOS/Android settings.
  • User Content: Event posters/photos and text you upload (stored in Firebase Cloud Storage/Firestore).
  • Device & Diagnostics: Basic device info, crash logs, and app performance data to keep StreetVine reliable.
  • Push Tokens: Anonymous device tokens to deliver event invites/notifications (via Firebase Cloud Messaging).

How We Use Information

  • Provide core features (discover, pin, share, and view events; see friends’ interest).
  • Show events near your chosen area and power filters/search.
  • Maintain safety, prevent abuse, and improve the app.

Our Approach to Ads & Tracking

StreetVine does not display third-party ads and does not sell personal data. We use only standard platform analytics and encryption.

Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted providers to operate StreetVine:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Messaging)
  • Google Places API (location search/autocomplete)

See Google’s Privacy Policy for details.

Security & Encryption

All network communication uses industry-standard TLS (HTTPS). Credentials and tokens are protected by the underlying OS (e.g., Keychain on iOS). No proprietary encryption is implemented.

Your Choices

  • Permissions: You can revoke camera, photos, or location access at any time in system settings.
  • Notifications: You can turn push notifications on/off in system settings.
  • Access/Deletion: You may request a copy of your data or deletion of your account.

Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion at any time.

Children’s Privacy

StreetVine is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Users

Your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our providers operate. We apply the same protections regardless of location.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted at this URL with the updated effective date.
